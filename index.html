What We Do

Every Detail of Your Kitchen, Handled

Nine specialized trades under one licensed contractor. One point of contact, one timeline, one team that owns the result.

01

Kitchen Design & Planning

Custom layouts, 3D visualization, material selection, and permit coordination tailored to your space and budget.

02

Cabinet Installation

Custom and semi-custom cabinets — soft-close hardware, crown molding, pull-out shelving, and perfect alignment.

03

Countertops

Granite, quartz, and marble fabrication and installation — templated on-site for a flawless fit.

04

Backsplash & Tile

Subway, mosaic, herringbone, natural stone — precision tile work that ties the whole kitchen together.

05

Plumbing & Fixtures

Sink installation, faucet upgrades, garbage disposals, dishwasher lines, and gas line connections.

06

Electrical & Lighting

Recessed lighting, under-cabinet LEDs, pendant fixtures, outlet additions, and dedicated appliance circuits.

07

Flooring

Hardwood, luxury vinyl plank, porcelain tile, and natural stone — installed flat, flush, and built to last.

08

Drywall & Painting

Patch, skim-coat, texture, prime, and paint — walls that look factory-new when we're done.

09

Permits & Inspections

We pull all required permits and coordinate city inspections — you never have to visit the building department.

Our Process

Five Steps to Your New Kitchen

From first phone call to final walkthrough. No guessing, no chaos — a clear path from old kitchen to new.

01

Free Consultation

We visit your home, measure the space, discuss your vision, and walk you through material options — countertops, cabinets, tile, fixtures. No pressure, no cost.

02

Design & Planning

We create a detailed plan with layout, materials, timeline, and transparent pricing. We handle permits so you don't have to.

03

Demo & Prep

Old cabinets, countertops, and flooring come out. We protect the rest of your home and prep the space for new work.

04

Build & Install

Cabinets, countertops, tile, plumbing, electrical, flooring — everything goes in with precision. We keep the site clean daily.

05

Final Walkthrough

We walk through every detail together. Touch-ups, adjustments, final inspection. You don't pay the balance until you're completely satisfied.
