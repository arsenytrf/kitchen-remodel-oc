What We Do

Full-Service Kitchen Remodeling

From the first sketch to the final detail, we handle every phase of your kitchen renovation. One contractor, one vision, one beautiful result.

Kitchen Design

Custom layout planning and 3D visualization to maximize your space. We work with you to design the kitchen you've always wanted.

Permits & Planning

We handle all city permits, inspections, and code compliance so your project stays legal and stress-free from start to finish.

Plumbing

Full plumbing services including sink installation, garbage disposal, dishwasher hookup, gas line relocation, and water line upgrades.

Electrical

Panel upgrades, new circuits, recessed lighting, under-cabinet LEDs, outlet relocation, and appliance wiring — all to code.

Cabinets

Custom and semi-custom cabinetry in every style — shaker, flat panel, raised panel. Soft-close hardware, lazy susans, and pull-out organizers.

Countertops

Quartz, granite, marble, and butcher block countertops. Precision templating and professional installation with seamless edges.

Drywall & Texture

Smooth finish, orange peel, knockdown — we repair, patch, and finish walls and ceilings for a flawless surface ready for paint.

Painting

Interior painting with premium finishes. Walls, ceilings, trim, and cabinets — clean lines and lasting color that transforms your kitchen.

Flooring

Hardwood, luxury vinyl plank, tile, and natural stone flooring. Subfloor prep, leveling, and expert installation for a finish that lasts.
