How It Works

Four steps to your new kitchen.

Most kitchen remodels take 4-8 weeks depending on scope. Here is exactly how the process unfolds so you always know what is happening and when.

01

Consultation

We walk your kitchen, listen to what you want changed, and take measurements. You get a written scope of work and transparent pricing -- no ballpark guesses.

02

Design & Permits

We finalize the layout, materials, and finishes. We pull all required city permits and schedule inspections so nothing holds up the build.

03

Build

Demolition through final install. Plumbing, electrical, cabinets, countertops, flooring, paint -- all handled by our crew. Daily cleanup, weekly updates.

04

Reveal

Final walkthrough with a punch list. We don't collect final payment until you're satisfied with every detail. Your new kitchen, ready to use.
