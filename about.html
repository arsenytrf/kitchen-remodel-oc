Our Story

Built on Craftsmanship,
Not Shortcuts.

For over 15 years, Misael has been transforming kitchens across Orange County. What started as a deep respect for quality carpentry grew into a full-service remodeling operation that handles every phase of a kitchen build — from the initial design concept to the final hardware installation.

We’re not a franchise. We’re not a “handyman with a truck.” We’re a licensed general contractor (#1152610) who pulls permits, coordinates inspections, and stands behind every inch of work.

Our approach is design-to-finish: one contractor, one vision, no finger-pointing between trades. When you hire us, you get someone who cares about the grain of your cabinets, the seam of your countertop, and the grout lines on your backsplash — because that’s what separates a remodel from a renovation that lasts.
