Our Story

Started with a hammer. Built a reputation.

Misael started in the trades over 15 years ago, learning every aspect of home construction from the ground up. Plumbing runs, electrical panels, cabinet fitting, drywall finishing -- he did not skip the hard parts.

After earning his General Contractor license (#1152610), he focused exclusively on kitchen remodeling -- the most complex room in any home. It is where plumbing, electrical, cabinetry, and finish work all converge in a tight space with zero margin for error.

Today, Kitchen Remodeling by Misael handles everything from small countertop-and-cabinet refreshes to complete gut renovations. Every project runs through Misael directly -- he is on-site daily, managing the work, coordinating trades, and making sure every detail is right before moving on.

“I don't send crews to do work I wouldn't do myself. If my name is on the license, my hands are on the job.”
