  • Custom & semi-custom options

  • Pull-out organizers

  • On-site templating
  • Undermount sink cutouts
  • Edge profile selection
  • Sealing & finishing

The backsplash is where personality meets protection. We install subway tile, mosaic, herringbone, natural stone, and decorative patterns. Every cut is precise, every grout line is clean, and the finished product ties your countertops, cabinets, and paint together seamlessly.

  • Subway & mosaic patterns
  • Natural stone tile
  • Herringbone & chevron
  • Precision cuts around outlets
  • Grout sealing

Electrical & Lighting

Good lighting makes or breaks a kitchen. We install recessed can lights, under-cabinet LED strips, pendant fixtures over islands, and dimmer switches. We also add GFCI outlets where needed and dedicated circuits for heavy appliances.

  • Recessed lighting
  • Under-cabinet LEDs
  • Pendant fixtures
  • GFCI outlets
  • Appliance circuits

  • Porcelain & ceramic tile

  • Moisture barriers
  • Seamless transitions
